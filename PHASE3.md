# ProseCraft — Phase 3 Roadmap

## AI Writing Features
- **AI Rewrite Suggestions on Click**: Click overused/flagged words in the Insights Panel to get inline AI replacement suggestions
- **Tone Shift Per Scene**: Set a target tone (e.g., suspenseful, romantic, comedic) and get AI feedback on how well each scene matches
- **Character Voice Profiles**: Define distinct voice patterns per character; AI flags when dialogue drifts out of character
- **Beat Sheet Generator**: Auto-generate story beats from a premise or synopsis, mapped to chapters/scenes
- **AI-Powered Continuity Checker**: Flag inconsistencies across scenes (names, timelines, settings, character details)

## Editor Enhancements
- **Distraction-Free Typewriter Mode**: Current line stays centered on screen while typing
- **Inline Comments / Annotations**: Add margin notes to scenes without editing the manuscript
- **Version History / Snapshots**: Save and restore named snapshots of any scene
- **Split-Pane View**: View two scenes side-by-side for reference while writing
- **Custom Fonts & Line Spacing**: Writer-configurable typography settings

## Dashboard & Organization
- **Manuscript Templates**: Pre-built structures (3-act, Hero's Journey, Save the Cat) as starting points
- **Tag / Label System**: Color-coded tags on scenes and chapters for tracking POV, subplot, status
- **Project-Level Word Count Goals**: Daily/weekly/total targets with progress tracking and streak tracking
- **Multi-Format Import**: Support .docx, .rtf, .epub import in addition to .txt
- **Multi-Format Export**: Export as .docx, .pdf, .epub with formatting preserved

## Beta Reader & Feedback
- **Adjustable Feedback Depth**: Let users choose feedback intensity (light touch, balanced, deep dive) to control how many annotations the Beta Reader returns
- **Full Manuscript Breakdown Report**: Submit the entire manuscript for a comprehensive editorial report — structure, pacing, character arcs, prose quality — delivered as a downloadable document

## Analytics & Insights
- **Pacing Analysis**: Visual graph of scene tension/word density across the manuscript
- **Readability Scoring**: Flesch-Kincaid and other readability metrics per scene and project-wide
- **Dialogue-to-Prose Ratio**: Track and visualize the balance across chapters
- **Writing Session Analytics**: Time-based stats — words per session, productivity trends, best writing times

## Collaboration (Future)
- **Shared Projects**: Invite beta readers or editors with read-only or comment access
- **Editor Feedback Mode**: Inline suggestions that the author can accept/reject
- **Real-Time Co-Writing**: Simultaneous editing with presence indicators
